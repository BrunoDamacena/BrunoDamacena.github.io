Rubik's Cube
Since December 2016, the Rubik's Cube has been one of my favorites hobbies. I always liked challenges and things that allow me to overcome myself, so the cube was an instant addiction to me. I looked up a method to solve it and learned it in one day. Since then, I've been collecting and learning to solve several different models of the puzzle. You can check out my profile at the World Cube Association, the official worldwide organization and regulator of Rubik's Cube competitions.
